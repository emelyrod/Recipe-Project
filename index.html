MAJARETE


Majarete ingredients

  • 4 cobs of husked, fresh sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup of sugar(or more,to taste)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 3 cups of whole milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • A pich of freshly-grated nutmeg(optional)

"Directions"

  1. prepare corn:shuck (remove husk and silk from) the corncobs. using a sharp knife cut kernels from cob about 2/3 the depth of the kernels.
  2. Making the pudding:Blend the corn kernels together with sugar,cornstarch,cinnamon powder, milk,salt and water. pass the mixture thorugh a strain and keep the liquid (discard the solid parts). Pour into a 3 quart [3 it]pot. add the cinnamon sticks. cook over over medum heat stirring constantly to avoid sticking. when it thicknes to the consistency of drinkable yogurt remove from the heat (15 mins approx). Taste and stir in sugar to taste if you find it neccessary.
